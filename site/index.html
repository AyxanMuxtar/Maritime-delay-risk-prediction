Monthly delay-risk forecast

Daily probability of weather conditions severe enough to disrupt maritime operations across five Caspian Sea ports.

How to read this

Low Probability < 25%. Normal operations expected.
Elevated 25–50%. Watch the weather; have contingency plans.
High > 50%. Disruption likely; review schedules.
Climatology For days beyond the 16-day forecast horizon, predictions use historical averages by calendar day. Lower confidence.

Methodology

Predictions are produced by an XGBoost classifier trained on ten years of ERA5 reanalysis weather data (2015–2024) for each port. The target is whether a given day will breach any of eight operational thresholds: storm-force winds, dangerous gusts, heavy precipitation, snow, fog, or wave-derived hazards.

For the upcoming month, days within the 16-day forecast horizon use real Open-Meteo forecast features fed through the model. Days beyond that horizon use a per-(city, day-of-year) climatology lookup smoothed over a ±7-day window. Each prediction is tagged with its source.

The full pipeline runs automatically on the 1st of each month via GitHub Actions. View source on GitHub →
